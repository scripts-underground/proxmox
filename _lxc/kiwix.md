Offline Wikipedia reader. Serves ZIM content (Wikipedia, Wiktionary, etc.) over HTTP.

## Notes

- Access at `http://<ip>:8080`
- Lightweight — runs on as little as 512 MB RAM
- Load ZIM files into `/var/lib/kiwix/` to serve content
